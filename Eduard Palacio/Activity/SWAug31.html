My favorite travel destination: Japan

The Land of the Rising Sun. A place rich of history and culture, and it’s no wonder it is loved by many.

6 Reasons Why I love Japan

  1. Anime

    2. Anime is a popular animation niche and style that originates from Japan and has started from the local gaming industry but since took over the world by storm.

  2. Video Games

    3. Video games are a major industry in Japan, and the country is considered one of the most influential in video gaming.

  3. Shrines

    4. Another thing that really interests me are Shrines. Japanese people pray for good fortune in various aspects of life and make offerings at the different shrines.

  4. Scenery

    5. Japan has some of the most breath-taking views one could ever dream of.

  5. Vending machines

    6. Just like convenience stores, you’ll always find those colourful vending machines, even in places where you’d least expect.

  6. Cleanliness

    7. In terms of cleanliness, no other country does it like Japan. Indeed, Japan is the most traveller-friendly countries in the world.
