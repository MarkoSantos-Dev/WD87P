Tim Cone to coach Gilas Pilipinas in Asian Games

Publised On:September 6, 2023

Tim Cone, the Philippine Basketball Associations (PBA) winningest head coach, will be calling the shots for Gilas Pilipinas in the 19th Asian Games, the Inquirer learned on Thursday.Barangay Ginebras revered head coach will be joined by his club deputy Richard del Rosario, according to sources.

Alfrancis Chua, the San Miguel Corporations sports director and the Gin Kings governor in the PBA Board, will be the squads manager.

Another source said the order came from San Miguels president and chief executive officer, Ramon S. Ang.

The Asian Games kicks off on September 23 in Hangzhou, China, with the basketball tournament tipping off three days later. Gilas, which finished fifth in the last edition held in Jakarta, Indonesia in 2018 under the guidance of Yeng Guiao, will open its campaign against Brunei.
