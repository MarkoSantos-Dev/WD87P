Lake Apo

Lake Apo is a crater lake located at Barangay Guinoyoran, Valencia City, Province of Bukidnon. The name of the lake come from the bisaya term 'apo', meaning 'elder or grandparent'. According to legend, there was a man living in the mountains who assaulted his granddaughter and was punished by the mountain deities for his disrespectful act by flooding the area forming the Lake Apo It is developed into a Public Park where tourist and locals can come to relax and enjoy the scenic views of Lake Apo.
